## 5. Color Palette

### Light Mode
- **Primary:** #6366f1 (Indigo)
- **Secondary:** #8b5cf6 (Purple)
- **Background:** #f8fafc (Slate 50)
- **Surface:** #ffffff (White)
- **Text:** #1e293b (Slate 800)
- **Border:** #e2e8f0 (Slate 200)

### Dark Mode
- **Primary:** #818cf8 (Indigo 400)
- **Secondary:** #a78bfa (Purple 400)
- **Background:** #0f172a (Slate 900)
- **Surface:** #1e293b (Slate 800)
- **Text:** #f1f5f9 (Slate 100)
- **Border:** #334155 (Slate 700)

---

## 6. Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings:** 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Body:** 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Monospace:** 'JetBrains Mono', monospace

### Font Sizes
- **H1:** 2.5rem (40px)
- **H2:** 2rem (32px)
- **H3:** 1.5rem (24px)
- **Body:** 1rem (16px)
- **Small:** 0.875rem (14px)

---

## 7. Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Indigo gradient, rounded corners
- **Secondary:** White with border
- **Icon:** Square, centered icon

### Cards
- **Background:** White (light) / Slate 800 (dark)
- **Border:** Subtle border
- **Shadow:** Soft shadow
- **Radius:** 12px

### Inputs
- **Background:** White (light) / Slate 700 (dark)
- **Border:** Subtle border
- **Focus:** Indigo ring
- **Radius:** 8px

---

## 8. Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile:** < 640px
- **Tablet:** 640px - 1024px
- **Desktop:** > 1024px

---

## 9. Animations

- **Page Load:** Fade in (500ms)
- **Hover:** Scale (1.05), Shadow increase
- **Loading:** Spinner, Skeleton screens
- **Toast:** Slide in from right

---

## 10. Accessibility

- **Contrast:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Focus:** Visible focus states
- **Labels:** All inputs have labels
- **Keyboard:** Full keyboard navigation
- **Screen Reader:** ARIA labels
